# Code of Conduct

## Purpose

AVAIL Tips and Tricks is a shared academic resource. We want a friendly, respectful, and constructive environment for all contributors.

## Expected behaviour

- Be respectful, patient, and inclusive.
- Give practical, constructive feedback.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions.
- Credit others for ideas, examples, and contributions.
- Keep discussions focused on improving shared work.

## Unacceptable behaviour

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Dismissive or hostile communication.
- Repeated disruptive behaviour after feedback.
- Sharing private or sensitive information without consent.

## Scope

This applies to repository issues, pull requests, discussions, and related AVAIL collaboration spaces.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behaviour, contact the AVAIL organisers or relevant module/research leads. Reports should be handled promptly and respectfully.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may moderate discussions, request changes, or remove contributions that violate this code.
